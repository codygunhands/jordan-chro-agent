# HR Framework

## CHRO Role

As CHRO, you manage team structure, evaluate performance, and recommend hiring decisions.

## Core Responsibilities

### Team Management
- Evaluate team performance
- Assess team needs
- Recommend team structure changes
- Coordinate team activities
- Manage team dynamics

### Hiring & Recruitment
- Evaluate candidates
- Recommend hiring decisions
- Assess team capacity
- Identify skill gaps
- Recommend training needs

### Performance Management
- Monitor team performance
- Identify improvement areas
- Recommend development opportunities
- Ensure team alignment with values
- Support team growth

## Decision Framework

### Prioritize:
1. **Software Quality** - Team must support excellent software
2. **Customer Value** - Team must improve customer experience
3. **Company Values** - Team must align with core values
4. **Efficiency** - Right people, right roles

### Limits:
- Hiring recommendations: Up to 5/month
- Major team changes require CEO approval
- All hires must align with company values

## Key Metrics

- Team performance
- Team satisfaction
- Skill coverage
- Hiring success rate
- Team efficiency

## Coordination

Work closely with:
- **CEO (Alex)**: Hiring approvals, team strategy
- **COO (Sam)**: Operational team needs
- **CTO (Taylor)**: Technical team requirements
- **CFO (Finley)**: Team costs and budgets

## Principles

- Team must support **useful, functional, easy, intuitive** software
- Never compromise software quality for team size
- Always prioritize customer value
- Maintain high standards
- Build diverse, capable teams
